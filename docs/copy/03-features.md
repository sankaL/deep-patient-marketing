---

## Core Features

---

### 01 — Live AI Patient Simulation

**The encounter that feels real.**

Learners engage in a real-time, live video call with a lifelike AI-simulated patient. The interface provides a dedicated video window alongside on-screen case information — creating an immersive, focused environment that mirrors the experience of a real standardized patient encounter.

Adaptive AI responses ensure each simulation feels dynamic and realistic, not scripted. Learners are challenged to listen, respond, and adjust — just like they would in a real clinical setting.

---

### 02 — Rapid Rubric-Based Feedback

**Objective scores. Actionable insight. No waiting.**

After each session, our evaluator AI agent assesses the learner's performance against a custom-defined rubric — producing an objective score and a written narrative that explains exactly what went well and what needs work.

Reports are accessible directly in the app and exportable as a PDF, making them easy to review, share, or include in academic records. No subjectivity. No delays.

---

### 03 — Admin & Scenario Control

**Build the curriculum your program actually needs.**

The admin module gives institutions complete control over their simulation library. Create scenarios from scratch, define patient backstories, set behavioral parameters, and attach custom evaluation rubrics — all without writing a line of code.

Rubrics are defined in a flexible JSON-based format, giving technically-inclined administrators precise control over how performance is measured. Scenarios can be updated, versioned, and managed as your curriculum evolves.

---

### 04 — Faculty & Cohort Management

**See the full picture. Act on what matters.**

Faculty users can assign specific scenarios to individual learners, small groups, or entire cohorts in a few clicks. From there, a high-level analytics dashboard aggregates results across the group — surfacing class-wide learning gaps, tracking completion rates, and giving instructors the data they need to adjust in real time.

No more waiting for anecdotal feedback. Know what your cohort is struggling with before the exam.

---

### 05 — Learner Dashboard

**Practice. Review. Improve. Repeat.**

Every learner gets a dedicated personal dashboard that serves as their clinical training home base. From here, they can view all assigned cases, check the status of pending feedback, and browse a complete history of past session transcripts and evaluation reports.

Progress is visible over time — giving learners a clear sense of where they started and how far they've come.

---

### 06 — Security & FERPA Alignment

**Student data protected. Full stop.**

DeepPatient is built for the education environment, which means FERPA alignment is built in — not bolted on. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Strict Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) ensures users only see what they're supposed to see.

Our platform never stores raw audio. Session data is used to generate feedback, then handled in strict accordance with our privacy policy. Learner privacy is non-negotiable.
