## Why We Price This Way

Standardized patient programs cost institutions thousands of dollars per cohort, every semester. DeepPatient is designed to reduce that burden — but the right pricing depends on how many learners you have, how often they practice, and what features your faculty need.

That's why we don't publish a one-size-fits-all price list. Instead, we work with your team to scope a solution that delivers real ROI and fits within your budget.

## What's Always Included

Every DeepPatient plan includes the full platform — no feature gating, no upsells:

- Live AI patient simulation (unlimited sessions, per plan)
- Rubric-based post-session feedback with PDF export
- Faculty scenario assignment and cohort analytics dashboard
- Admin scenario and rubric management
- Learner personal dashboard and progress tracking
- FERPA-aligned data handling, encryption in transit and at rest, RBAC
- Onboarding support and scenario setup assistance

## Have Questions Before You Reach Out?

**FAQ**

**Is there a free trial or pilot option?**
We offer structured pilot programs for qualifying institutions. Reach out and let us know you're interested in a pilot — we're happy to discuss what that looks like for your program.

**How long does onboarding take?**
Most institutions are up and running within two to four weeks. Our team handles scenario configuration, user onboarding, and initial training for admins and faculty.

**Can we use our own evaluation rubrics?**
Yes. Rubric design is entirely yours. Our team can help translate your existing clinical assessment frameworks into the DeepPatient format.

**Is DeepPatient compatible with our existing LMS?**
We support integration discussions on a case-by-case basis. Reach out and we'll let you know what's possible.
