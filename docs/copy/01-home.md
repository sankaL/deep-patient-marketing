## What is DeepPatient?

DeepPatient is a complete clinical skills training platform built for every role at your institution — from secure scenario creation to cohort-level analytics.

Learners get a judgment-free space to practice at their own pace. Faculty get the oversight, data, and control they need. Admins get a system that scales.

**Three pillars:**

- **Lifelike AI Conversations** — Engage in real-time interviews with adaptive AI patients designed to behave like the real thing.
- **Rubric-Based Feedback** — Get instant, objective evaluations powered by LLMs, built on rubrics your faculty define.
- **Scalable & Cost-Effective** — Dramatically reduce the cost and logistics of standardized patient programs without sacrificing quality.
