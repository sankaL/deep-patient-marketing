# Our Features

DeepPatient is an end-to-end solution for clinical skills training. From a lifelike, real-time simulation experience to powerful administrative controls and asynchronous feedback, our platform is built to be scalable, secure, and effective. Explore the core features below.

## Live AI Patient Simulation

Medical practitioners engage in a real-time, live video call with a lifelike, simulated virtual patient. The platform provides a dedicated video interface, complete with on-screen case information, for a realistic and immersive practice experience.

![Gemini_Generated_Image_uuswqpuuswqpuusw-removebg-preview.png](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/f91ee9_ab4ca460871746d78a0f13d57d182dd0~mv2.png/v1/fill/w_287,h_287,al_c,q_85,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/Gemini_Generated_Image_uuswqpuuswqpuusw-removebg-preview.png)

## Rapid Rubric-Based Feedback

After each session, our evaluator AI agent assesses the learner's performance against a custom-defined rubric, providing an objective score and narrative feedback. The final report is accessible via the application and available to export as a PDF.

![Gemini_Generated_Image_xn13lqxn13lqxn13-removebg-preview.png](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/f91ee9_be5e31f5188945d19a6498ebe885f8e7~mv2.png/v1/fill/w_371,h_355,al_c,q_85,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/Gemini_Generated_Image_xn13lqxn13lqxn13-removebg-preview.png)

## Admin & Scenario Control

A powerful admin module to manage all users, including medical practitioners and faculty members. Admins have complete control to createscenarios, define patient backstories, and specify unique evaluation rubrics.

![Gemini_Generated_Image_1a0lae1a0lae1a0l-removebg-preview.png](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/f91ee9_b30f9532e0044b2c960b2b669aa0f8a0~mv2.png/v1/fill/w_287,h_287,al_c,q_85,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/Gemini_Generated_Image_1a0lae1a0lae1a0l-removebg-preview.png)

## Faculty & Cohort Management

Faculty users can easily assignspecificscenarios to individual learners, small groups, or entire cohorts with a few clicks. A high-level analytics dashboard provides aggregated results to identify class-wide learning gaps.

![Gemini_Generated_Image_2u0g72u0g72u0g72-removebg-preview.png](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/f91ee9_e7834a8e0f3045b98eea0f14ed88b236~mv2.png/v1/fill/w_294,h_294,al_c,q_85,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/Gemini_Generated_Image_2u0g72u0g72u0g72-removebg-preview.png)

## Learner Dashboard

A dedicated, personaldashboard for learners (medical practitioners). Here users can view all assigned cases, track the status of pending feedback, and access a complete history of their past transcripts and reports to monitor improvement.

![Gemini_Generated_Image_dv8l51dv8l51dv8l-removebg-preview.png](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/f91ee9_e0612fcfac9f4d46a39dbb1f266af26f~mv2.png/v1/fill/w_295,h_314,al_c,q_85,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/Gemini_Generated_Image_dv8l51dv8l51dv8l-removebg-preview.png)

## Security & FERPA-Alignment

Built for education, the platform is FERPA-aligned. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, with strict Role-Based Access Control (RBAC). Our policy never stores raw audio, ensuring learner privacy.
